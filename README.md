## AI Platforms & Tools

### Bulldog Genie

Evolved from an Alexa-style campus assistant into a GenAI-enabled, agentic infrastructure that supports institutional knowledge discovery, task guidance, and scalable service enablement. Bulldog Genie integrates enterprise systems, curated knowledge sources, and governance controls to deliver reliable, role-aware AI experiences for students, faculty, and staff.

*Bulldog Genie serves as the foundational backbone for digital transformation agents such as **TechVisor** and **Eduvisor**, enabling consistent architecture, shared governance, and reusable AI capabilities across multiple institutional use cases.*

**2025 Highlights**

- Expanded functionality, use cases, and adoption based on user feedback and evolving AI capabilities.
- Established a reusable GenAI infrastructure supporting multiple digital transformation agents.
- Implemented governance-aligned controls for trusted knowledge access and role-aware experiences.
- Built a scalable integration foundation for secure, enterprise-grade AI services.
- Adopted cloud-native DevSecOps practices to ensure repeatable deployments, security review workflows, and maintainable engineering operations.

## Change Management, Enablement & Self-Service

This workstream focuses on building confidence, competence, and consistency in AI adoption through structured onboarding, communications, training, and scalable access models.

### Onboarding & Enablement Content (Campus-Facing)

Developed and maintained structured onboarding pathways, guidance, documentation, and web resources to support safe and effective use of AI tools, including ChatGPT and other emerging platforms.

## Agentic AI for Digital Transformation

*These initiatives represent **Agentic AI solutions** designed to optimize workflows and modernize services through AI-guided task assistance, trusted knowledge retrieval, and scalable adoption patterns. All agents are designed to align with shared infrastructure and governance controls.*

### Eduvisor (Agentic AI for Academic Advising Exploration)

Explores AI-guided academic advising and student support workflows through structured assistance and trusted knowledge access. Collaborated with campus partners to define use cases, identify process improvement opportunities, and evaluate readiness for adoption.

### TechVisor (Agentic AI for Technology Guidance)

Explores AI-guided technology decision support and service advancement through consistent, scalable assistance for campus users. Partnered with Technology Services - User Support and Service Desk to develop use cases that reduce support friction, improve service pathways, and strengthen campus technology enablement.
