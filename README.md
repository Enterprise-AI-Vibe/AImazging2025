## AI Platforms & Tools

### Bulldog Genie

Evolved from an Alexa-style campus assistant into a GenAI-enabled, agentic infrastructure that supports institutional knowledge discovery, task guidance, and scalable service enablement. Bulldog Genie integrates enterprise systems, curated knowledge sources, and governance controls to deliver reliable, role-aware AI experiences for students, faculty, and staff.

*This platform serves as the foundational backbone for digital transformation agents such as **TechVisor** and **Eduvisor**, enabling consistent architecture, shared governance, and reusable AI capabilities across multiple institutional use cases.*

**Highlights of 2025**

- Expanded functionality, use cases, and adoption based on user feedback and evolving AI capabilities.
- Established a reusable GenAI infrastructure to support multiple digital transformation agents (e.g., TechVisor, Eduvisor).
- Implemented governance-aligned controls for trusted knowledge access and role-aware experiences.
- Built a scalable integration foundation to support secure, enterprise-grade AI services.
- Adopted cloud-native DevSecOps practices and automation to ensure repeatable deployments, security review workflows, and maintainable engineering operations.
